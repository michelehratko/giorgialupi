Gender Equality Creative Platform

A branded data visualization system helps tell human stories about gender equality. Every element is constructed out of an equal sign (=) or a plus sign (+), reinforcing the centrality of these two concepts to the pursuit of meaningful gender parity.

Subject: Nonprofit and social impact
Medium: Editorial, campaign
Year: 2021
Client: Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation
