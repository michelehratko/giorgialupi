Data Items: A Fashion Landscape

A data-driven installation created for MoMA interprets the exhibition, ‘Items: Is Fashion Modern?’ The designers wanted to dig deeper, to understand why each item had such significance and what criteria underpinned the curators’ choices.

Subject: Arts and culture, history, fashion
Medium: Exhibition
Year: 2017
Client: The Museum of Modern Art
