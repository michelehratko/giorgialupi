Making data accessible by making it wearable on garments that incorporate Lupi's hand-drawn data patterns. The patterns are inspired by the lives of three trailblazing women: Ada Lovelace, or “The Mathematician,” recognized as the first computer programmer; Rachel Carson, “The Environmentalist,” the writer and conservationist who helped pioneer the environmental movement; and Mae Jemison, “The Astronaut,” the physicist and former NASA astronaut who was the first African-American woman in space.
